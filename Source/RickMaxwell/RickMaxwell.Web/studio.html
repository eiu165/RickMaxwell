The Process of "Cold Compression"

Tools and Materials

For me the process has always had a stronger allure than standing in front of the finished piece. I love the tools I work with, the materials I work in, and the empathy I experience as I move through the process.

studio work | installation

Touch Point Twist

I am interested in the negative space between the ribbons of wood. TOUCH, two 40 foot sculptures, closely touch each other representing the feminine and masculine... the "recepticle" and "point" of positive and negative space. In POINT and TWIST... I see in the wood's twists and movements of line and spaces between the lines of the wood.
